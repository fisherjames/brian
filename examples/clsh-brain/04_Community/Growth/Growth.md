# Community Growth — clsh.dev

Strategies for growing the open source community.

## Growth Funnel

```
clsh.dev landing (demo) → GitHub repo → Star → Discord → Contributor
```

## Growth Tactics

1. **Build in public** on X — share progress, get early followers
2. **Show HN** launch — big initial spike
3. **Product Hunt** — reach developer audience
4. **Reddit** — r/programming, r/webdev, r/commandline, r/SideProject
5. **Dev.to articles** — technical content driving traffic to repo
6. **Discord invite in README** — convert stargazers to community members
7. **Good first issues** — convert community members to contributors
8. **Screenshot-worthy UI** — the MacBook frame is inherently shareable

## Viral Mechanics

- "Show your setup" screenshots of the MacBook terminal
- Share demo link (works without backend)
- Mobile terminal screenshots are novel and attention-grabbing
