# vision

> Part of [[product/index]]

Brian is a control surface for an AI-native company.

The CEO should be able to:
- set intent,
- inspect real discussion and decision context,
- approve or deny explicit questions,
- supervise execution and merge gates,
- and ship outcomes without opening an IDE.

The UI should optimize for:
- high signal over noise,
- visible reasoning over hidden state,
- governance over ad hoc automation,
- and confidence in what changed and why.
