- Founders and product/engineering leads running AI-assisted delivery.
- Teams that require transparent reasoning and auditable changes.

Brian turns AI delivery from opaque chat threads into a visible operating system with markdown records, workflow gates, and mission control.

## Differentiation
- Markdown is the source of truth.
- Organisational layers are explicit (CEO, tribe, squad).
- Decisions and escalations are first-class, not hidden in logs.
- Human verification is a required merge gate.
